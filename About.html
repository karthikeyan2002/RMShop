ABOUT

This website will be useful in identifying a nearby mechanic shop so that it will be easy for the users to repair their vehices instantly.

The locations of the various mechanic shops in and around the current location will be displayed on thegoogle map along with their contact details.You will be able to check that contact details in the google map according to your needs and contact them for immediate service.

This application has an advantage of giving 24x7 service.

This will be helpful for the users who are new to the city

Services Available

Battery Change

We need to change the battery often and if we don't have a backup while travelling on the road contacting a nearby mechanic immediately will solve this.

Engine Repairs

Sometimes due to unexpected heavy rain, our engines could get repaired at the middle while riding our vehicles. So for replacing the engines immediately we could check the location of a mechanic who is near your current location and contact them immediately.

Oil & Break Checks

Sometimes we might face oil leakages or oil needs during our travel and we may not have a backup. At that time this remote mechanic will help you to reach out a nearby mechanic shop.

Tires Fix & Replacements

If your tire is punctured at some location while you are travelling, you could check a nearby mechanic according to their availability and contact them for helping you. They will reach out to you in minutes for replacements and tires fixing.

Tow Truck

A tow truck is a truck used to move disabled, improperly parked, impounded, or otherwise indisposed motor vehicles. If we have to carry our vehicles to the home, we could check for a mechanic shop were tow trucks are available.

Vehicle Maintenance

To maintain your vehicle cleanly from your own place or home without reaching out to a mechanic shop. An instant step could be taken using this website to find and contact your nearby mechanic.
